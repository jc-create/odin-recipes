Korean Ground Beef

Recipe Description

This is a quick and easy ground beef recipe that requires very little preparation.

The ground beef is typically served over rice, however, feel free to serve it over vegetables, noodles, or however you like! The beauty with cooking is that nothing should ever be set in stone.

Allergies Warning: contains sesame and soy.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup reduced/low sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red-pepper flakes, more or less depending on spicyness
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1-2 green onions, thinly sliced, for garnish
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame seeds, for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, red pepper flakes, and ginger.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high head. Add ground beef and good until almost browned, crumbling the beef as it cooks. Drain excess fat with a papertowel, then add garlic and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  3. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and continue to brown the ground beef to desired tenderness, around 2-5 minutes.
  4. Remove from skillet, serve over rice, and garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions, if desired.
